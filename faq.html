Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How does CheapFlyer find deals?

We aggregate prices from over 500 airlines and travel providers in real-time. Our algorithms compare millions of fare combinations to find you the best prices.

Is there a booking fee?

No, CheapFlyer never charges booking fees. The price you see is the price you pay.

Why do you need my location?

We use your location to show personalized deals from your nearest airport. This helps us display relevant flights and save you time searching.

Can I book one-way flights?

Yes! Simply leave the return date field empty when searching for flights.

How do I cancel a booking?

Cancellation policies vary by airline and fare type. Visit our Cancellation Policy page or contact us for assistance.

Are prices guaranteed?

Prices are subject to availability and may change based on airline inventory. We recommend booking quickly when you find a great deal.

Do you offer price alerts?

Yes! Subscribe to our newsletter to receive deal alerts for your favorite destinations.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your payment details on our servers.
